Our Story

A coastal gem on
Sliema's shoreline

Perched on Tower Road, where the morning light catches the rippling waters of Sliema Creek, Mason's Bistro has long been a beloved fixture on Malta's dining scene. We opened with one belief: that beautiful food is best enjoyed with sea air and good company.

Our kitchen draws on the sun-drenched flavours of the Mediterranean, from the olive groves of Greece to the spice markets of North Africa, interpreted through Malta's own rich culinary heritage. Every plate carries the warmth of the islands it was inspired by.

Whether you're joining us for a lazy brunch watching the ferries drift past, cocktails at our famous happy hour, or a long dinner as the harbour lights come on. Mason's is where Sliema slows down.
